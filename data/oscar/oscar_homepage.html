Welcome to the Sports Capital Programme Portal


NOTICE:

The Community Sport Facilities Fund is temporarily closed for registrations in order to facilitate migration to a new system. Organisations that are currently registered on OSCAR will automatically transfer to the new portal. The new system portal will be launched and open to new registrations in the coming weeks. Further details of this will be published here and communicated broadly. If you have recently submitted a registration application form, the Department will contact you directly regarding the next steps. Thank you for your patience.


LATEST POSITION:

The latest round (2023) of the Community Sport Facilities Fund (formerly Sports Capital and Equipment Programme) closed for applications on Friday, 8th September 2023.

A list of all provisional allocations is available at - 2023 grants.

In line with previous rounds of the Fund, a review of the current funding round has been undertaken and will be published shortly. The Minister has asked the Department to prepare for a further round of the CSFF in 2026. It is anticipated that the next CSFF round will particularly target areas and sports that have been under-invested in over the years.

This portal is used to apply for funding and manage the drawdown of grants. Please avoid using the firefox browser.
